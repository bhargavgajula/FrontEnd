In the quiet of twilight's embrace,
Where shadows dance with gentle grace,
The wind unveils its whispered tale,
A story spun in a soft, rhythmic hail.

Through rustling leaves, secrets are sown,
In nature's hush, a symphony is known.
Branches sway in a mystical dance,
A waltz of whispers, a fleeting trance.

Moonlight weaves through the silken air,
A celestial spotlight, tender and rare.
Stars above, silent witnesses they keep,
To the lullaby that the night winds reap.

Listen close to the wind's soft song,
A melody ancient, enduring and strong.
It tells of journeys, of love untold,
In the language of the breeze, stories unfold.
